Creative Profile

Pianist, Composer, and Producer celebrated for a trailblazing six-finger technique developed after a career-threatening injury. His 30-year journey spans jazz, contemporary classical, and high-end electronic production.

One of the most singular and daring compositional voices of his generation. He creates immersive soundscapes and scores for global brands including Armani, Prada, Moschino, and Audi — works where music, visual art, and new technologies become a single experience.

A pioneer in the use of artificial intelligence as an expressive language, he was awarded 'Best AI Short Film' at the 2025 AI Film Awards in Paris.

His is a life crossed by a single musical prism: from jazz played alongside Miles Davis and Dizzy Gillespie, to 1990s New York hip hop with Q-Tip and Redman, through to Lenny Kravitz and the most extreme frontiers of electronic music with Skrillex and Wiwek. Music has no genre. Music is the genre.
