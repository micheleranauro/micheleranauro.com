Creative Profile

Pianist, Composer, and Producer celebrated for a trailblazing six-finger technique developed after a career-threatening injury. His 30-year journey spans jazz, contemporary classical, and high-end electronic production.

He creates immersive soundscapes and high-impact scores for global luxury brands including Armani, Prada, Moschino, and Audi. His live experiences merge music with visual art and new technologies.

As a founding partner of WICK Productions (Milano/New York), he integrates multimedia storytelling for Hollywood clients. He was awarded 'Best AI Short Film' at the 2025 AI Film Awards in Paris.

Notable validations include playing alongside Miles Davis and in concert with Dizzy Gillespie. He has also collaborated with contemporary icons like Skrillex, Wiwek, and Davide Rossi.
